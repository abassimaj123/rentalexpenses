## English

### Title (max 30 chars)
RentalExpenses: Landlord Calc

### Short Description (max 80 chars)
Track rental expenses by category. NOI, expense ratio, PDF export. EN+ES.

### Long Description (max 4000 chars)
**RentalExpenses** is the landlord's expense calculator — track every cost category for your rental property, see your NOI and expense ratio, and generate a clean PDF report. No bank sync required.

**Expense categories tracked:**
- Property taxes
- Homeowner's / landlord insurance
- Maintenance and repairs
- Property management fees
- HOA dues
- Utilities (water, trash, lawn, etc.)
- Mortgage interest (P&I)
- Vacancy allowance
- Capital expenditure (CapEx) reserve
- Advertising / leasing costs
- Legal and accounting fees
- Other / miscellaneous

**What it calculates:**
- Monthly expense total by category
- Annual expense total by category
- Gross rental income
- **Net Operating Income (NOI)** — income minus operating expenses (excluding mortgage)
- **Expense ratio (%)** — total expenses as a percentage of gross income
- Net cash flow after mortgage

**Key features:**
- Category-level breakdown — see which expenses are eating your returns
- Monthly and annual views — toggle between periods
- Detailed history — save full expense profiles for each property
- PDF export — year-end expense reports for tax prep or lenders
- 100% offline — no bank sync, no account, no subscription
- Bilingual: full English + Spanish support

**Who it's for:**
- Landlords with 1–10 rental properties
- Buy-and-hold investors tracking actual vs. projected expenses
- Investors preparing year-end reports for taxes
- Anyone who wants to know their true NOI without spreadsheets

**This is NOT bookkeeping software.**
RentalExpenses does not import bank transactions or track individual receipts. It is a structured expense calculator — enter your costs by category, get your NOI and expense ratio instantly.

**Why RentalExpenses beats the competition:**
Full-featured property management platforms (AppFolio, Buildium, Rentec) cost $1–$3 per unit per month and are overkill for small landlords. Spreadsheets are error-prone. RentalExpenses is the middle ground: structured, fast, and purpose-built for expense analysis without the overhead.

Add PDF export and bilingual support, and it is the most useful landlord expense tool on Android.

**Privacy:** All calculations are local. No financial data is transmitted. Anonymous analytics via Firebase only.
