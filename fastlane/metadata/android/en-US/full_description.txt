The landlord app that actually saves you money at tax time. Track all your rental properties, income, and expenses in one place — and generate a complete Schedule E summary ready for your CPA or tax software at year-end. Designed for individual landlords who self-manage between 1 and 20 properties without paying for expensive property management software.

Multi-property management dashboard shows all your properties at a glance with occupancy status, monthly rent, and last payment date. Tenant tracking with payment logs records every rent payment, late fee, and partial payment — with a running ledger per tenant so disputes are settled with data, not memory. Recurring expense tracking handles mortgage payments, insurance, HOA fees, utilities, lawn care, and any regular cost on a monthly, quarterly, or annual schedule. One-time expenses for repairs, appliances, and capital improvements logged with receipt photo capture directly in the app — point your camera at any receipt and it's stored with the expense record, ready for an audit.

Schedule E tax summary automatically categorizes your expenses into IRS Schedule E line items: advertising, auto and travel, cleaning and maintenance, depreciation, insurance, legal and professional fees, management fees, mortgage interest, repairs, supplies, taxes, utilities, and other. Property comparison tool ranks your properties by cash flow, ROI, and cap rate so you know which ones are performing and which ones need attention. ROI calculator shows cash-on-cash return, total return including appreciation, and GRM (gross rent multiplier). PDF export of all reports for sharing with your accountant.

Full bilingual support in English and Spanish (EN+ES). Dark mode, works offline — no cloud subscription required.
